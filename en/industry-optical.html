Why Optical Communication
Manufacturers Choose Jian Chang

The exact machining challenges of optical communication metal parts are where Jian Chang's Swiss-type turning excels.

  • Ferrule OD is typically Ø1.25–2.5mm with ±0.001–0.003mm tolerance — a natural fit for Swiss-type guide-bushing turning, delivering stable roundness and batch-to-batch consistency.
  • Proven high-volume production in SUS316, SUS440C, and brass C3604 — stainless steel for corrosion resistance, brass for machinability, 440C for wear resistance, selected per application.
  • Cam automatic lathes support mature part numbers at 100,000+ pcs/month with fast cycle times, low cost, and stable lead times.
  • Cleaning, deburring, sampling, or 100% full inspection can be integrated — parts arrive ready for direct assembly.
  • From Ø0.7mm micro locating pins to Ø20mm flange housings, one supplier covers the full range — simplifying supply chain management.

Material Properties Suitable Parts Notes
