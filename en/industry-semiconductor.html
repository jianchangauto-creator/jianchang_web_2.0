Recommended Materials for Semiconductor Equipment Parts

Comparison of material properties and suitable applications for semiconductor equipment parts.

Material Properties Suitable Parts Notes
SKH9 (High-speed Steel) High hardness, wear resistant Locating pins, wear parts Suitable for high-frequency contact wear
SUS440C High hardness, dimensionally stable Precision locating pins Can reach higher precision with grinding
SUJ2 (Bearing Steel) High hardness, wear resistant, dimensionally stable Sliding bushings, shaft parts Common for precision rolling/sliding elements
Aluminum (6061 / 7075) Lightweight, machinable Fixture bodies, structural parts Hard anodizing improves wear resistance
SUS304 Corrosion resistant, non-magnetic Vacuum chamber parts Common non-magnetic material in semiconductor equipment
