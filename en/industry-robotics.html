Why Robotics & Automation Makers
Choose Jian Chang

The exact challenges of automation equipment part machining are where Jian Chang's Swiss-type turning excels.

  • Swiss-type turning is particularly suited to high L/D ratio long-shaft machining, delivering stable roundness and straightness — core requirements for robot joint and drive shafts.
  • Proven production experience with SCM435, SCM440, and SUS304, selected per load and corrosion-resistance requirements.
  • Machining range of Ø1–20mm covers everything from robot joint shafts to sensor mounting brackets.
  • Heat treatment can be coordinated to improve hardness and fatigue resistance, per customer drawing and specification.
  • One supplier from prototype through production — simplifying part-number and supplier management complexity in automation equipment supply chains.
