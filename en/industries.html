Semiconductor Equipment

Precision Parts for Semiconductor Equipment

Wafer handling, alignment, and fixturing equipment require large quantities of precision locating PINs, spacers, and fasteners — all with extremely tight dimensional consistency.

Jian Chang's capability with high-hardness materials such as SKH9 and SUS440C, combined with proven mass production of precision PINs, directly meets this industry's requirements.
